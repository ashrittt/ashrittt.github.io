How do I breathe,
without you here beside me?
Every beat within my chest
whispers your name, I find no rest.

You’re the air I choose to breathe.
As nights grow cold, I still believe.
Like a statue, I stand so true,
lost, just staring — thinking of you.

The stars still swirl in front of me,
in the same room where we used to be.
Even if dawn begins to rise,
I'm trapped beneath your endless skies.

How do I breathe, when I can’t lie
beside you, as the days go by?
Your presence lingers — soft and slow —
in melodies that come and go.

The heat still thumps like beating drums,
a rhythm deep — then silence comes.
A shiver climbs along my spine
when I can almost feel you — mine.
- A.L
