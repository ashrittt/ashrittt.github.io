･ﾟ✧:･ ABOUT ME ✩ ₊˚

Trisha Jane Carla L. Campos


19 years old | Quezon City | 2nd year student of FEU-TECH
Bachelor of Multimedia Arts
Specialization: Traditional and Digital Arts, Media, and Animation.
Great in: Hosting, Drawing, Painting, Writing, and Designing.

Director of Programs | Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS)
Hosting Committe | PRISM, the official "mother-org" of Multimedia Arts Community.

My experience in writing takes back to when I was still a child. Along with Art, I have another hobby, and it's reading tons of novels, and writing them in my own style. I have tried once become a writer in Wattpad,
and a writer in Facebook during pandemic, yet I stopped due to my busy schedule and interest. But, even so I'm not writing long stories, I kept writing poems, love letters for crushes, as they served as my journal, where my feelings are aligned with short stanza and verses, hoping to reach out my lonely self. I also write short stories, publish them in platforms I hide my identity. Overall, my love for writing is littlebut it is still there, and I want to improve it more.

I'm proud to say that I'm a Writer, Poet, an Artist, Reader, and a Dreamer.
