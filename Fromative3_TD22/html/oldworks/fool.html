One day I walked to the point of nowhere,
Blind and mute — just breathing air.
Tripping on my own worn shoes,
Lost in thoughts I couldn’t use.

But do you ever wonder if I can survive?
It’s a question that keeps me alive.
To be in a narrow, tightening space,
Where escape’s the only saving grace.

Yet still, I whisper, I’ll reach the sky —
“In the binds that hold me, can’t be the reason I can’t fly.”
- A.L
