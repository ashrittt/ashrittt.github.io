Titled: Sorry, I like you too

There was a boy I liked, but he doesn't know. He was like a bully to me for some reason, as he will
do some things that can lead me to anger. But what can I do? I can only be angry for a moment but as
I saw those wide smile he has, and those bright eyes, I can only sigh and think, "How did I fall in
love with person?"

One time, when he blocked my way at the corridor where no students around, I confronted him, asking,
"Why are you doing this? Is it fun to see me like this?"

He looked at me with a smile, "Well, I want to see your angry face because only with this, I can
grab your attention."

I looked at him confusedly, which he answered, "I like you." With the serious bright eyes he had,
there's no trace of lies behind.

"Are you pranking me?" I asked carefully. I don't have any hopes for him to like me, I'm not that narcissistic.

"No. Seriously, I like you a lot. I like you every seconds and hours, day after day, I still like
you." He said.

With embarrassed face, I immediately kicked him at the stomach. "Sorry.." I said, patting his back
after that kick.
He looked at me sadly, "Was I..." Before he can continue his words, I immediately shut his mouth with a kiss, and said to him,

"Sorry, I like you too."

-A.L


Titled: Wildest dreams

I'm staring on the sunset while sitting on the tree trunk. I was wondering why would this happen to
me? Does my fate really that miserable? My eyes began to tear up when I dazed back to the past. You
were the most dazzling person I met, and you're the only one who I loved. But there's a thin wall
separates us, causing us to have conflicts. But I never thought, that thin wall can completely
destroy our love. Should I love you more that time? Should I just break that thin wall and come over
to your side so I can be with you more? If only I have one more chance, a chance that can make
myself drag to your arms one more. To change our fates. But I can only wish on the stars or in my
dreams, as I saw you holding other woman's hand.

Until the sun sets complete, and only my sobbing can be heard in this lonely dark night. If I can
only bet and wish on my wildest dreams, to make you remember me, and our past, or to turn back to
the time so I can save you that night.

-A.L
