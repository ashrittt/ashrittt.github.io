Saw you standing by the school staircase,
My heart was lost in your gentle gaze.
Never thought I’d fall again,
Should I gamble my love — once again?

We met in moments I never planned,
Yet they linger, warm as your hand.
But what can I do if it fades someday?
Maybe you’ll smile before you walk away.

Still, I’m down to gamble my heart for you,
Just to keep up with all that you do.
I want you so much — I’m scared you’ll stray,
But I promise to stay… I will stay.
- A.L
