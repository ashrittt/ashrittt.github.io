Memories flash inside my head,
Cold winds dry the tears I’ve shed.
Blaming myself for what I’ve done,
For losing us when we’d just begun.

I feared the words I’d hate to hear,
The truth that now feels crystal clear.
And in that silence, my heart did break—
The greatest, saddest, small mistake.

If time would let me turn once more,
I’d ask the things I failed before:
“What went wrong? What did I do?
Can I still make it right with you?”

I’m sorry for the pain I gave,
For love I didn’t know how to save.
Selfish, careless — I can’t deny,
Yet still I hope you’ll see me try.

No longer yours, though it’s hard to hide,
I still wish to stay right by your side.

- A.L
