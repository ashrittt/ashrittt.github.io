Think how I only want your number in my phone,
How our eyes first met — I was starstruck, overthrown.
Think how butterflies go wild inside of me,
When you put your arms around me gently.

I’ve always liked the night — the moon, its glow,
The quiet peace the darkness seems to show.
But when you said you loved the sunsets too,
I started chasing every hue for you.

I captured each one, let the colors shine through,
Painting the sky — always thinking of you.
