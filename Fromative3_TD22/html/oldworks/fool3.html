Fluttering waves in the sunset, I held my hand in the air and seemed lost.
"What should I do?", I asked,
knowing no one would answer me.

As I walked through the sand, and heard the wails of waves,
I ran my fingers through my hair with the gust of wind blowing in my face,
And I,.. will stare at this scenery,
"So helpless", I said

Helpless on the things I can't manage,
and escape was my only method,
to breathe in relief,
away from problems.
It's good once for a while, experiencing this.
- A.L
