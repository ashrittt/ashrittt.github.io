A Writer

Someone asked me,
“Are you a writer?”
I replied,
“If it takes a person
to write meaningful words
that reach someone’s attention,
then yes, I am.”
– A.L

Fool


One day I walked to the point of nowhere,
Blind and mute — just breathing air.
Tripping on my own worn shoes,
Lost in thoughts I couldn’t use.

But do you ever wonder if I can survive?
It’s a question that keeps me alive.
To be in a narrow, tightening space,
Where escape’s the only saving grace.

Yet still, I whisper, I’ll reach the sky —
“In the binds that hold me, can’t be the reason I can’t fly.”
- A.L

Don't Give Up


Don’t give up when life feels hard to bear,
Don’t give up when days are filled with fear.
’Cause whenever the storms appear,
hey’ll shape your strength and make it clear —
In every pain you’ve battled through,
It’s painting brighter shades in you.

Colors of the wind in the skies,
Contrasts of bright and dark collide.
It may seem that joy’s disguise,
But I know it’s my reason to fly from the lies.

Within me burns a heart so strong,
I’ll live my life, my whole life long.
I feel my heartbeat, fast and bold,
A fire of dreams I’ll never fold.

So don’t give up on your own fight,
Trials and errors come to dim your light.
But falling down won’t mean you’re small,
’Cause every time you rise, stand tall.
- A.L

How Do I Breathe


How do I breathe,
without you here beside me?
Every beat within my chest
whispers your name, I find no rest.

You’re the air I choose to breathe.
As nights grow cold, I still believe.
Like a statue, I stand so true,
lost, just staring — thinking of you.

The stars still swirl in front of me,
in the same room where we used to be.
Even if dawn begins to rise,
I'm trapped beneath your endless skies.

How do I breathe, when I can’t lie
beside you, as the days go by?
Your presence lingers — soft and slow —
in melodies that come and go.

The heat still thumps like beating drums,
a rhythm deep — then silence comes.
A shiver climbs along my spine
when I can almost feel you — mine.
- A.L
